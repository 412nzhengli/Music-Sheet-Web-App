Your Music Teacher is free tool for all
newcomers to the instrumental world of music.

Many people want to learn how to play unconventional songs that do not have existing or easily accessible sheet music (music from tv shows, anime, EDM, other countries) but they do not have the skills to transcribe music simply from listening.
Your Music Teacher solves the hassle of looking for sheet music online as some can be quite hard to find, while for other songs there are none available.

Guide
Pick up an instrument, and be excited to learn!

1. Music Teacher works best with clear, single layered tunes.
2. Either select an audio file from your computer or drag it on to the box.
3. Once you have selected your audio, click the 'Analyze!' button and Music Teacher will generate the notes alongside your audio!
